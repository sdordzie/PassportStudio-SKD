How to Use PassportStudio Pro
Step 1 — Upload
Click the upload zone or drag & drop your photo. Supports JPG, PNG, WEBP. You can also use your camera on mobile or paste from clipboard.
Step 2 — Edit
Adjust brightness, contrast, saturation. Rotate, flip, or change the background color. Use undo/redo to step through changes.
Step 3 — Crop
Choose a preset (Passport, Visa, ID) or enter custom dimensions. Drag the crop box. Lock/unlock aspect ratio. Hit "Apply Crop".
Step 4 — Layout
Choose paper size (A4, Letter, 4R, etc.). Set rows, columns, margins, and gaps. Cut lines and borders are optional.
Step 5 — Export
Download as PDF, PNG, JPG, or print directly. You can also export just the cropped photo or a Word-compatible doc.
🔒 Privacy
All processing is 100% local. No image is ever uploaded to any server.
